The Panther

In Jardin des Plantes, Paris

... is a poem by Rainer Maria Rilke, written between 1902 and 1903. The event described in it was based on a panther that Rilke saw during his visits to the Jardin des Plantes in Paris. It is a touching poem with powerful images.

Humanity

... is a poem by Georg Trakl. He describes the hopelessness that befell him during the World War I. As a medical Lieutenant, he had to care for numerous seriously wounded people in the Battle of Gródek in September 1914, but was unable to save their lives, which plunged him into deep despair.
